Tourist Attractions in Shariatpur

Some of the major attractions of this district are:
Fatehjung Fort: The fort was built by the Mughal commander Raja Mansingh. When Raja Mansingh attacked Bikrampur, his fellow warriors were defeated by the King Kedar Rai and took refuge in Srinagar. Mansingh sent his troops to rescue them. As a result, a massive battle took place. Kedar Rai got injured in this battle and died. The fort of Mansingh is a mark of the battle.
Burir Hat Mosque: The Burir Hat Mosque in Sadar sub-district is very famous and a great symbol of Islamic architecture.
Modern Fantasy Kingdom: This is an amusement park located in Kedarpur union. Here has the largest aquarium of Bangladesh.
Other attractions are Lakarta Sikder House, Ponditshar, Hatturia Palace etc.
